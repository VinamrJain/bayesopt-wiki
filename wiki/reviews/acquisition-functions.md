- [acquisition-functions-2] gap (open) — The four-families table (line 74) labels the Information/lookahead 'Scores x by' as 'entropy of p_⋆ for ES/MES'. Per notation.md, p_⋆(x|D_n) is the posterior distribution over the maximizer LOCATION. MES (max-value entropy search) targets the entropy of the optimum VALUE f*, not p_⋆ (location) — that is the defining ES-vs-MES distinction, and the note's own body (lines 51-54: 'location x*, or value f*') states it correctly. The table collapses both onto p_⋆, mislabeling MES. In the table 'Scores x by' cell, distinguish: entropy of p_⋆ (location) for ES/PES vs entropy of the optimum value f* for MES — e.g. 'entropy of p_⋆ (ES/PES) or of f* (MES)'.
