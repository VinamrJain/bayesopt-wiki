- [knowledge-gradient-3] nit (open) — The note attributes KG's positive value for measurements that lift the posterior-mean max 'even when the sampled value is no better than the current best' generically, and grounds the noise-free 'only a small improvement over EI' claim on both frazier2009kg and frazier2018. This is correct, but frazier2009kg's own empirical basis for 'small in noise-free' is specific (its Figure 3 second experiment: difference 'quite small but still statistically significant', biggest in early iterations; the first noise-free comparison found NO statistically significant difference because the optimum was found in the first stage).
